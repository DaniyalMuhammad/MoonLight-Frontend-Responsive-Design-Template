Courses We Offer

Moon Light School offered the following diciplines in English medium.

Primary Education

Our school provides education to students in their early years of formal schooling, typically ranging from kindergarten or preschool through elementary school grades (such as first grade to fifth or sixth grade, depending on the education system). We focus on laying the foundation for a child's academic, social, and emotional development.

Secondary Education

School offering secondary studies typically refers to an educational institution that provides education to students in their later years of formal schooling, often referred to as middle school or high school. Secondary education typically covers grades ranging from sixth or seventh grade through twelfth grade, depending on the education system.

Higher Education

A school offering higher studies typically refers to a college, university, or graduate school that provides advanced education and degree programs beyond the undergraduate level. These institutions offer various postgraduate degrees such as master's degrees and doctoral degrees.
